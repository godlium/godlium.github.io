## RMG型回转式格栅除污机

## Type RMG Rotary Mechanical Grille

### 应用场合Applications

一般用作为污水预处理的第二道（或第二道以后）格栅，作细格栅使用，最小间隙可达1mm。还可用于纺织、印染、屠宰、制革造纸等工业废水的固液分离。

It is generally used in sewage pretreatment the second stage (or after the second stage) as a fine grate that minimum clearance can be l mm. It can also be used for solid-liquid separation in textile, printing and dyeing, abattoir, leather and paper and other industrial effluents.

### 工作原理 Working Principle

RMG型回转式格栅除污机是由一种独特的耙齿装配成一个旋转耙齿面，耙齿面在电机减速器的驱动下，进行逆水流方向的回转运动。耙齿链组在设备的顶部回转时，每组耙齿之间通过导轮和弯轨产生相对的自清运动，使绝大部分固体物质在重力作用下落入栅渣收集斗，另一部分残留污物，则依靠清扫装置清扫干净。

RMG rotary screen decontamination machine is assembled by a unique toothed rake in a rotary harrow tooth surface, toothed rake in the motor drive, the direction of reverse water rotation movement. As the harrow tooth chain group rotates towards the top of the machine, the relative self-cleaning movement between each group of harrow teeth is produced by the guide wheel and bending rail by means of a cleaning device.
